# maximum*reactangle*surface

Determine the coordinates of the rectangle with the maximum surface that one can place in a 2\*dimensions surface populated with obstacles (`X`), e.g.:

```
* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
*           X                      X                *
*     X * * * * * * * * * * * * *                   *
*       *                       * X           X     *
*       *                       *                   *
*       *                       *       X           *
*       *                       *                   *
*       * * * * * * * * * * * * *                   *
*           X                   X                   *
*                                           X   X X *
*                       X                 X         *
* X                              X                  *
*       X                                           *
* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
```
